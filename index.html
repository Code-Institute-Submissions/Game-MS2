Find The Heavier Ball

There are nine identical basketballs and one is heavier than the rest. Can you find the heaviest ball?

Follow these steps:

  1. Drag the basketballs one by one on to both sides of the scale.

  2. Click on the "S" button to check your selection. You can only click this twice so choose wisely. The scale should move if you chose the heaviest ball. CLick the scale button to rebalance the scale.

  3. To discard a light ball, drag and drop into the bin. This step is optional.

  4. If you think you found the heaviest ball, drag it to the net and click the "A" button to find out if you chose the heaviest basketball.
    NOTE: This can only be done once.

You have 180s to complete the riddle. Click to start. Good Luck!
